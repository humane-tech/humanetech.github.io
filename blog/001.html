I failed

2018 April 23

Some time in early 2015 I set out to become the person I wanted to be. In the process, I learned a lot. My perspective, mind and life changed in ways I'm incredibly grateful for. This is an overview to the key resources of my transformation. On many of these ideas

Where to start?

I planned to build a multimillion-dollar network marketing business. Made 200 bucks, lost a thousand. I planned to buy a Mercedes on my 20th birthday. I ended up walking to school. I tried to stop smoking. Failed at least 10 times. I planned to be a millionaire at age 21. That didn't work. I planned to create and grow finrthings, an online business. Didn't earn a dime. I created and wrote a blog for an event startup of a friend of mine. Wrote 20 blogposts, posted 57 Instagram posts. We stopped working on it to problems with the programmers and other venues. Got billed 300€ for not giving credit to a photographer. I set out to meditate 365 days in a row. Made it to 19 days. I wanted to reach my weight-goal of 75 multiple times. That did not work. I tried to sell t-shirts online. Spent 200€ on Facebook marketing, ended up selling 3 shirts. I set out to learn react native. Got not much further than 'Hello World.'
